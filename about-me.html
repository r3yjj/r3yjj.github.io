About me

Hi, my name is Ray, currently a student, studying in both Ngee Ann Poly (NP) and Institute of Technical Education (ITE).

I’ve always dreamed of traveling around the world to explore different cultures and lifestyles. Growing up in Singapore — a small island — the things I’ve experienced here are quite limited. I truly believe there’s so much more out there waiting to be discovered, and I hope that one day, when I’m financially stable, this dream will come true.

During my free time, I enjoy listening to and singing songs — especially V-pop, T-pop, K-pop, C-pop, and English pop. Music helps me feel good both mentally and emotionally. I also love taking time to rest as part of my self-care routine. Resting keeps me stress-free and gives me a chance to recharge without having to think about anything. As an animal lover, I also wish that one day I’ll be able to have a pet — preferably a rabbit — so that I can spend more of my free time caring for and bonding with it.

I have many interests in life, but one that stands out is my dream of starting my own business someday. Ever since I was young, I’ve aspired to run a business in the skincare and makeup industry because it’s something I’m truly passionate about. Although I haven’t started yet — mainly due to my busy school schedule and lack of capital — I hope that once I’ve completed my studies, I’ll be able to focus on building my business and turning that dream into reality.
